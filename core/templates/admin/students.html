ℹ️ How Automatic Semester Transitions Work

Semesters advance automatically when their end date passes. At that moment the Reset Agent will: (1) compile final stats into the History table below, (2) reset all student responses so they retake the chatbot, (3) delete all appointments and events, and (4) switch the current semester to the next one. You will see a notification on this dashboard when a transition occurs.
